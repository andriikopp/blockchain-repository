How to earn?

2 Provide details of a BPMN model you want to share (title, link, hash as the SHA256 value, brief annotation, and industry name). Put "0" to the "payableAmount" field.
3 Click on the "Write" button. Make sure your Web3 wallet (e.g. MetaMask or another one) is connected.
4 Soon after a transaction is mined you will receive a reward in ETHBPMN.
